What is Executive Functioning?

Executive functioning refers to the cognitive skills that people use to complete tasks. Neurodivergent individuals (people with ADHD, Autism, etc.) often struggle with executive functioning. Thankfully, there are lots of things you can try at home to help your child overcome some of these challenges!

How do I use the Parenting Playbook?

  • Not sure where to start? Users can take a quiz to find out which areas of executive functioning their child may need extra supports with!
  • You can also search by executive functioning domain, intervention, or by keywords.
  • Join our community by creating an account!
  • Users have the opportunity to find potential strategies to try at home save them to an account.
  • Community members can also add interventions to the database that other users can access and comment on strategies they have tried in the past.

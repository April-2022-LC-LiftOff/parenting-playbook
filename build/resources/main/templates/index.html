What is executive functioning?

Executive functioning refers to the cognitive skills that people use to complete tasks. Neurodivergent individuals (people with ADHD, Autism, etc.) often struggle with executive functioning. Thankfully, there are lots of things you can try at home to help your child overcome some of these challenges!

This site is an excellent resource for:

  • Parents
  • Caregivers
  • Teachers

Welcome to the Parenting Playbook

We are a community of parents dedicated to sharing effective practices that will increase children's executive functioning skills. These strategies can help child improve their behavior, increase their confidence, and give them the tools they need to succeed!

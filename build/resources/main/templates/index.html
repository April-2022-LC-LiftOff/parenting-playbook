We are a community of parents dedicated to sharing strategies to improve neurodivergent children's executive functioning skills!

Executive Functioning Information:

Executive functioning refers to the cognitive skills that people use to complete tasks.


Domains refer to different components of executive functioning.


Tags are used to group similar strategies together.


Neurodiversity refers to people who have cognitive processes that differ from the norm. This includes people with:

  • ADHD
  • Autism
  • Mood disorders or other psychological disorders
  • Dyslexia, Dyscalcula, and Dysgraphia
  • Intellectual disabilities
